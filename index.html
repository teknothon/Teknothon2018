About the event

Growing from a humble beginning in 2011, Teknothon is the annual techno-cultural festival which promotes liberty, energy, enthusiasm and provides students with the opportunity to explore and rediscover themselves. Coupled with the legacy of fellowship and pride, Teknothon is one of the largest collegiate festival celebrations hence one of the most eagerly anticipated events in the University calendar. A programme of intriguing events will be held in a variety of venues at the heart of the campus, with something to cater to all tastes. With over 20+ events spanning across dynamic venues over 2 full days, Teknothon is an exhilarating experience from beginning to end.

With the rise in the number of participants, we strive to render the festival better every coming year by maintaining cordial relations with corporate community ensuring sponsorship for the fest and extensive publicity for the company.
